The DorfMovies rating control — five ★ with amber fill and half-star precision.

```jsx
<StarRating value={3.5} onChange={setRating} />
<StarRating value={4} readOnly size={16} showValue={false} />
```

Use the interactive form in cards/modals; `readOnly` (often smaller, `showValue={false}`) inside compact poster cards. Steps are 0.5.
